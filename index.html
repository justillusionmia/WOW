WOW Sessions Portfolio

Author: Timyah Whiskey - Pierre


Introduction

The World of Work (WOW) Sessions were a valuable tool for me as a student because it can be quite difficult to succeed in today's job market. These sessions were created to provide students like me with the opportunity to investigate several career paths and gain the skills they need to succeed.

Highlights

We participated in sessions every week that provided us with knowledge about the working world and how to better ourselves. I was very inspired by the chance to learn from people who had experienced these things before since it made it easier for me to see how what we learn in school is used in the real world.

Reflection

The best thing about each session, in my opinion, was how it was planned to actively include us. Instead of just listening to lectures, we participated in discussions, hands-on activities, and group projects that promoted collaboration and critical thinking. For me, this made learning much more interesting. I feel as if these sessions help to improve skills like good communication, teamwork, and problem-solving that are necessary in any career path.

This portfolio includes the fifteen WOW Sessions that took place throughout terms one and two, providing an overview of each lesson. As I reflect on these experiences, I feel more confident and prepared to pursue my career goals, knowing that I have gained a lot of knowledge.
